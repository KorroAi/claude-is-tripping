# Claude Is Tripping

<img src="assets/illustration.png" alt="Claude Is Tripping" width="100%">

> Three Claude agents. Four rounds. Escalating intensity. One idea that survives total annihilation.

**Claude Is Tripping** is a multi-agent psychedelic brainstorm engine for Claude Code. It doesn't just generate ideas — it spawns three agents locked in a recursive dialectical loop (thesis → antithesis → synthesis) with escalating intensity, each round making the ideas wilder and the destruction more brutal, until something emerges that no single mind could produce.

This is not a persona skill. It's an **orchestration skill**. Claude becomes the Trip Guide — spawning agents, managing dosage, escalating intensity, and judging when the trip has produced something that transcends what exists.

https://github.com/KorroAi/claude-is-tripping

## What It Actually Does

Every brainstorming method — human or AI — hits the same ceiling: the ideas come from one mind's distribution. Even multi-agent setups just run parallel Claude instances and pick the best output. Same latent space, different sampling.

Claude Is Tripping breaks that ceiling by introducing **adversarial creation**. The Visionary creates. The Destroyer annihilates. The Synthesizer finds the third thing — the idea that exists in the space between, dodging every fatal flaw while keeping the original ambition. Then it loops. And escalates. And at Round 4, everyone swaps roles.

The final idea was not sampled from a distribution. It was FORGED by conflict.

## How It Works

The Trip Guide spawns three agents per round across four escalating phases:

| Round | Name | Intensity | What Happens |
|-------|------|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | **Come Up** | Tipsy (0.3) | First thesis-antithesis-synthesis. Baseline established. |
| 2 | **Peak** | Wasted (0.7) | Escalation. Wilder connections. Brutal destruction. Desperate synthesis. |
| 3 | **Ego Death** | Blackout (1.0) | Complete dissolution. Total vision. Total annihilation. Transcendence. |
| 4 | **Reversal** | Blackout (1.0) | Roles swap. The Visionary destroys their own creation. The Destroyer builds from ashes. The Synthesizer judges. |

After the loop: **Integration** — a sober agent translates the breakthrough into one concrete, actionable paragraph. What Kevin actually DOES this week.

### The Agents

| Agent | Role | Energy |
|-------|------|--------|
| 🧃 **Visionary** | Generates the WTF idea | Drunk + Creative, "bro hear me out" |
| 💀 **Destroyer** | Finds every fatal flaw | Aggressive Drunk + First Principles |
| 🌀 **Synthesizer** | Finds the third way | Philosophical Drunk + Synthesis |

### The Quality Gate

The trip doesn't end until the Destroyer runs out of ammo:

1. Would a single Claude have found this? → REJECT, keep tripping
2. Can Kevin implement this week? → REJECT, re-Integrate
3. Does the Destroyer still have valid flaws? → REJECT, run another round

## Why It's Different

- **Not cosmetic.** Not a persona. Not a tone change. A structural change in how ideas are generated.
- **Not parallel.** Agents don't work independently and get synthesized. Each one reads the previous output. The chain creates emergence.
- **Not safe.** The Destroyer is not polite. If an idea survives, it's because it actually works — not because nobody wanted to criticize it.
- **Has an end condition.** The trip stops when destruction fails — not when the user gets bored.
